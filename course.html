The CRS SUPA Course

I am posting about the Public Speaking course in order to document my progress throughout it. This course seemed very interesting but also a bit challenging going in. I was most concerned about the quizzes, since I was never a great test taker. I felt confident in my ability to give speeches, but I worried I might talk too much!

For my first assignment, I had to curate a speech tied to both myself and an object that had some meaning to me. I chose a Rubik's Cube — to showcase how my mind works, how I prefer engaging with a puzzle over reading a boring long text. You can even tell, I mean I am coding this blog right now!

My goal is to get better at public speaking and be able to stand in front of a crowd without getting nervous. I also want to organize my thoughts, think on the spot more professionally, and most importantly, learn the best ways to convey a message effectively — maybe even get an audience to act on it.
